How Can College Students Live a Zero-Waste Lifestyle on Campus Without Spending More?

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Sustainability often feels expensive, especially for college students. But living a zero-waste lifestyle does not require costly eco-friendly products—it requires small behavioral changes. Simple campus-friendly practices include: Carrying reusable bottles and containers Reducing hostel food waste Sharing books, notes, and supplies Using digital notes instead of printing Practicing waste segregation The biggest waste problem is not lack of resources—it is lack of awareness and habits. Enactus promotes sustainability that is accessible and affordable. By focusing on behavior change instead of expensive alternatives, students can reduce waste without increasing expenses. A zero-waste campus is built through collective action, consistency, and conscious choices—not money.
