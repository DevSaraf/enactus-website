How Can College Students Live a Zero-Waste Lifestyle on Campus Without Spending More?

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Sustainability often feels expensive, especially for college students. But living a zero-waste lifestyle does not require costly eco-friendly products— it requires small behavioral changes.

Simple campus-friendly practices include carrying reusable bottles, reducing hostel food waste, sharing resources, using digital notes, and practicing waste segregation.

A zero-waste campus is built through collective action, consistency, and conscious choices—not money.
