What Is Social Entrepreneurship?

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Social entrepreneurship is the practice of using innovative business models to solve social and environmental problems. Unlike traditional entrepreneurship, the primary goal is impact, not profit alone.

Social entrepreneurs identify gaps in society—such as lack of education, healthcare, or sustainability—and design solutions that empower communities instead of relying on charity.

By blending purpose with entrepreneurship, social ventures create long-term, scalable, and financially sustainable impact.
