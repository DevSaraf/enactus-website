Social Entrepreneurship

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Social entrepreneurship is the practice of using innovative business models to solve social and environmental problems. Unlike traditional entrepreneurship, its primary goal is not profit, but creating sustainable impact while remaining financially viable. Social entrepreneurs identify gaps in society—such as lack of education, healthcare, or environmental sustainability—and design solutions that empower communities rather than depend on charity. By blending purpose with entrepreneurship, social entrepreneurship enables long-term change that is scalable, inclusive, and impactful.
