Students and Enactus

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Enactus provides students with a platform to grow beyond classrooms by working on real-world social and environmental problems. Through hands-on projects, students learn how to identify issues, design sustainable solutions, and implement them at the grassroots level. Enactus encourages leadership, teamwork, and innovation by placing students in situations where decision-making and accountability matter. By collaborating with communities, mentors, and industry professionals, students develop communication and problem-solving skills that are difficult to gain through academics alone. Enactus also exposes students to entrepreneurial thinking, helping them understand how impact-driven initiatives can be financially sustainable. Overall, Enactus plays a vital role in shaping confident, socially responsible individuals prepared to lead meaningful change.
