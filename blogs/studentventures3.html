Startups by students as side ventures

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Some of the most impactful social innovations have come from student-led startups. These ventures often begin as small college projects but grow into scalable solutions addressing education, healthcare, sustainability, and employment.

Students bring fresh perspectives, creativity, and a strong sense of purpose, allowing them to challenge traditional systems effectively. Student-led startups prove that age or experience is not a barrier to creating impact.
