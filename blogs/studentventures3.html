Startups by students as side ventures

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Some of the most impactful social innovations have come from student-led startups. These ventures often begin as small college projects but grow into scalable solutions addressing education, healthcare, sustainability, and employment. Students bring fresh perspectives, creativity, and a strong sense of purpose, allowing them to challenge traditional systems effectively. Student-led startups prove that age or experience is not a barrier to creating impact. With the right guidance, teamwork, and intent, students can build solutions that positively influence communities. These examples inspire others to believe that meaningful change can start within college campuses and expand far beyond them.
