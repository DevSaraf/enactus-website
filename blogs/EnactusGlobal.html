Is Enactus Just a College Club? Understanding Its Global Model

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Enactus may appear to be a college club, but it is actually a global platform for social entrepreneurship.

Operating in over 30 countries, Enactus connects students, academics, and business leaders through impact-driven enterprises.

Enactus is a global movement that prepares students to become future change-makers.
