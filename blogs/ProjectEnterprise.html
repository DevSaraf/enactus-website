The Difference Between a Project and an Enterprise (And Why It Matters)

By Enactus MUJ | December 2025

Many students use the terms “project” and “enterprise” interchangeably—but the difference is crucial.

Projects are short-term and often end when funding or deadlines finish. Enterprises, however, are designed to generate income and continue operating long-term.

At Enactus, students are encouraged to build enterprises because lasting impact comes from continuity, scalability, and ownership.
