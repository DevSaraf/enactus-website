Our Flagship Events

Sangam is the annual flagship cultural initiative of Enactus Manipal University Jaipur, where ideas, cultures, and communities converge. More than just a fest, it is a national gathering of Enactus clubs from across India, uniting young innovators and changemakers on one stage. Rooted in social innovation and Rajasthan’s heritage, Sangam blends impact-driven projects with vibrant performances, making it one of MUJ’s most significant and celebrated events. With Enactus MUJ’s proven record of driving sustainable, student-led initiatives, Sangam has become a festival of creativity, collaboration, and lasting impact.

About Us

Enactus is an international non-profit organization that empowers students to use entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of life and create a better world for others. It is a global network of students, academics, and business leaders who work together on social and environmental projects, aiming to achieve the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. Enactus provides a platform for students to develop leadership skills through a team-based, experiential learning model that includes projects and competitions.
